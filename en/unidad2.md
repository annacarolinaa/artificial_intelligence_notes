### Tools and Technologies

- **Python**: main language for analysis and modeling.  
- **Pandas, NumPy**: data manipulation and cleaning.  
- **Scikit-learn**: classification algorithms, cross-validation, and pipelines.  
- **Matplotlib, Seaborn**: data visualization and exploratory analysis.  
- **Jupyter Notebook**: documentation of experiments and reports.

### Unit Objective

- Explore advanced classification techniques in machine learning.  
- Compare different algorithms and evaluate their performance.  
- Apply models in practical contexts, with a focus on results interpretation.
