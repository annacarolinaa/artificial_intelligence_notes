This repository gathers reports developed during the Artificial Intelligence training,
with focus on the practical application of theoretical concepts in real contexts.

The works explore from initial data analysis stages to the construction,
evaluation and interpretation of machine learning models.

---

<h2>

The presented projects aim to:

- Consolidate fundamental Machine Learning concepts  
- Apply preprocessing and exploratory data analysis techniques  
- Develop and compare regression and classification models  
- Evaluate performance using appropriate metrics  
- Interpret results critically and grounded

### Unit 1 - Regression

- **Financial Inclusion**: Factors influencing financial inclusion.  
- **Happiness**: Exploration of well-being and satisfaction indicators.  
- **Obesity**: Study of obesity risk factors.  
- **Academic Performance**: Prediction of academic performance.

### Unit 2 - Classification

- **Logistic Regression and Cross-Validation**: Binary classification with robust evaluation.  
- **LDA and Decision Trees**: Dimensional reduction and decision making.  
- **Ensemble Models, SVM and Neural Networks**: Advanced classification techniques.  
- **Financial Inclusion Project**: Financial inclusion analysis with classification models.
